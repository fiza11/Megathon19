IOT - Drainage Control Dashboard

Red and Green Nodes

Nodes that are triggered to indicate overflow/ blockage are red in colour. Flow meter nodes are triggered red when the flow is low or varied indicating sudden changes in water level or accumulation of water. Manhole nodes are triggered red when the level of water is higher then what is normal/allowed.
